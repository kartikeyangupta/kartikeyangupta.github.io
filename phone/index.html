               When I was 8 years old my dad bought a personal computer . He used to work at evening so every morning I used to rush to the computer room after taking my breakfast and play computer games a lot (Specially : Sky Roads)

 

One day an incursion of thoughts struck me about how these games run on my pc and who makes them?

 

From that day the quest of becoming a professional programmer started .

I joined the computer club in school and started learning technologies/ languages like BASICS , Flash, HTML , etc.

Due to love towards gaming I started learning Graphic Designing Software like Autodesk Maya , Adobe Photoshop , Adobe AfterEffects when I was at ninth.

 

Currently doing my Graduation from Shri Ramdeobaba College of Engineering and Management , Nagpur .

 

 I am a verbose person and loves to create Machine Learning , Artificial Intelligent Software solution for real life problems.

 

 A photographer by hobby I love to explore places , learn new languages ,solve coding contests problems and create memes.
